SSC/CBSC

To prepare for SSC/CBSC exams, one must have an in-depth understanding of subjects like Mathematics, Science, Social Studies and Languages. Algebra, geometry, trigonometry and arithmetic are some topics covered under mathematics. Physics, chemistry and biology make up science which is based on fundamental concepts as well as practical applications. History, geography civics and economics fall under social studies; the emphasis is on historical happenings, geographical features political systems and economic theories respectively. Grammar proficiency, comprehension skill and literature analysis are required to pass language subjects. This involves going through recommended text books in order to get concepts right while attempting previous years’ question papers helps in familiarizing with exam patterns thereby leading to effective time management during the actual test. These examinations evaluate students’ knowledge of these topics plus their comprehension to help them advance further academically or begin working professionally
